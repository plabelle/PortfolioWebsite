Responsive

My approach to responsive web design is to develop applications that respond to the user's behavior and environment based on screen size, by making use of flexible layouts, flexible images and cascading style sheet media queries. I believe the best approach is a design that is tested in a wide range of devices to create uniformity and seamlessness for a good user experience.
